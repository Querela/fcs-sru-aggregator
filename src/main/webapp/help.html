Welcome to the CLARIN-D Federated Content Search

CLARIN-D Federated Content Search makes European linguistic resources accessable via the common interface. Currently, 9 centers in Germany and Netherland make more than 20 resources available to the lingustic researches, and this number is growing.

To perform simple keyword search in all available resources, go to the search field at the top of the page and click 'search' or press the 'Enter' button.

To select specific resources based on their name or language and to specify number of search results to be returned, click on the 'Advanced search' link.
