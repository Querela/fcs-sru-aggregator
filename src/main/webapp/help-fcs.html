To perform simple keyword search in all CLARIN-D Federated Content Search centers and their corpora, go to the search field at the top of the page and click 'search' button or press the 'Enter' key.


To select specific corpora based on their name or language and to specify number of search results per page, click on the 'Search options' link. Here, you can filter resources based on the language, select specific resources, set the number of hits per page request.


To save the search result, in the 'Search results' page, go to the menu and select either 'Export to Personal Workspace' or 'Download'. This menu appears only after all the results on the page have been loaded. Currently, the results can be saved either in CSV or TCF format.
